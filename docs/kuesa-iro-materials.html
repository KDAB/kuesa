    Kuesa Iro Materials

    The Kuesa Iro Materials are a set of simplified materials that can be used to extend glTF files. They are in most cases replacement for the glTF PBR material. There are specifically developped for embedded targets where the use of the more complex PBR material would harm performances.

    These materials have been implemented through the Kuesa custom material glTF extension mecanism . The Kuesa Addon for Blender 2.8 allows to create, preview and export the Iro materials.

    Available Iro Materials

    • IroDiffuseMaterial
    • IroDiffuseHemiMaterial
    • IroGlassAddMaterial
    • IroMatteOpaqueMaterial
    • IroMatteAlphaMaterial
    • IroMatteSkyboxMaterial
    • IroMatteBackgroundMaterial
